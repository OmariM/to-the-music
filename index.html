The Parties

Every night at To The Music ends on the dance floor. We're collaborating with local NYC club organizers, party promoters, and tastemakers to curate nights that feel at home in the city's dance music scene — not just a festival afterthought. Each party is designed to blend the hustle community with the energy of NYC nightlife.

Party lineups and venues are announced closer to the month of the festival. Follow @tothemusicnyc for updates.

Code of Conduct

Creating a safe, inclusive, and joyful space for everyone.

To The Music is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for all attendees, regardless of gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or dance level.

Consent is essential in partner dancing. Always ask before dancing with someone, and always respect a "no" gracefully. On the dance floor, be mindful of your partner's comfort and physical boundaries.

We do not tolerate harassment, intimidation, or disrespectful behavior in any form — on the dance floor, in workshops, at social events, or online. This includes unwelcome physical contact, inappropriate comments, and taking photos or videos without consent.

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code, please speak to any member of the organizing team immediately. We take all reports seriously and will respond promptly to ensure everyone feels safe.
